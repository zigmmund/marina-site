Location

Vilamoura, in the Algarve is an extremely luxury area full elegance and good taste and Marina Square shopping centre is privileged located due to its proximity to all these “mandatory” passage points for people who visit it.

Algarve

Filling the South of Portugal, the Algarve covers the south coastline. The region has an average of 300 sunny days a year and its climate is distinctive for the long warm summers and the short mild winters, making the Algarve a year-round tourist destination.

Having been considered the best beach destination in Europe by the World Travel Awards, the Algarve also has 4997 km2 of experiences to offer.

Vilamoura

Situated in the Golden Triangle area and only 20 min from the Faro airport, Vilamoura is one of Portugal’s most popular holiday resorts, a blend of luxury hotels receiving annually around 500.000 visitors per year, offering not only a very pleasant weather but also a wide range of water and land activities all with excellent infrastructures. The “relaxing” Vilamoura has several of the regions leading spa’s, two of the finest beaches in Europe and the larger Marina of Portugal.

Architect

Vasco Vieira was born in Johannesburg, South Africa, in 1969. In 1994 after completing his degree in Architecture at the University of the Witwatersrand (WITS) he moved to Portugal where he soon after found himself working in the prestigious resort of Vale do Lobo in the Algarve.

After a few years Vasco had been appointed as both Chief Architect and Construction Director of the Resort, a position that he kept until eventually deciding to set up his own practice. ARQUI + was set up mid 2003 and Vasco was immediately rewarded for the quality of his architecture by being listed as one of the top 10 architects internationally by the Homes Overseas Magazine in 2004. Since then ARQUI + has received a number of awards for their innovative designs.
